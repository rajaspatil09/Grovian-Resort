About the Stay

The Dormitory at Grovian Resort is designed for large groups, corporate teams, friends, and student outings who are looking for a comfortable and economical stay.

The dormitory offers clean shared spaces and full access to all resort activities, making it ideal for group experiences in a natural environment.
